How to Make Sugar Cookies

  1. Ingrediants

    • Sugar
    • Butter
    • Flour

  2. Prep Work

    1. Wash your hands
    2. Set ingrediants out
    3. Preheat the oven to 450

  3. Making the Cookies

    1. Put dry ingrediants in a bowl and mix
    2. Once mixed, add butter and vanilla
    3. Roll into balls and put into the oven for 15 minutes

  4. Decorating

    1. Spread icing atop your cookies
    2. Give it sprinkle eyes and a smiley face
    3. Give your cookie hair using icing and sprinkles
